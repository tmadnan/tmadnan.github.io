We introduce a large-scale multi-task video dataset (3,306 videos from 845 participants) and a novel fusion model (UFNet) for multimodal PD screening using webcam video. UFNet significantly outperformed single-task models in diagnostic accuracy (AUROC ~93%), demonstrating scalable at-home detection.
